# CLI Overview

---

- [`jovay network`](jovay-network.md): Manage network configurations, such as updating rpcs, or switching between chains.
- [`jovay wallet`](jovay-wallet.md): Manage wallets, check balances, and perform on-chain actions like transfers and approvals.
- [`jovay transaction`](jovay-transaction.md): Send raw transactions, or inspect the status of on-chain transactions.
- [`jovay contract`](jovay-contract.md): Call functions, or read state directly with on-chain contracts.
- [`jovay dapp`](jovay-dapp.md): Scaffold new DApp projects from built-in templates and manage their compilation and deployment.
